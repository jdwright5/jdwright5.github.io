How to Eat a Plant Based Diet

What does plant based mean?

A plant based whole food diet has numerous different health benefits. Be it medical, mental, or physical health, there's heaps of information supporting the fact that plant based, whole food eating can help to fight chronic diseases, improve mental health, and improve endurance. This website will serve as a resource to inform readers about the impact of eating a plant based diet, and provide information that will aid readers to make the switch such as recipes and food recommendations.

What chronic diseases are we talking about?

  • Heart Diseases
  • Lung Disease
  • Brain Diseases
  • Various Cancers
  • High Blood Pressure
  • Liver Disease

These are some of the most common diseases according to the CDC
