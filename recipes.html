Two of my Favorite Plant-Based Recipes

Cauliflower Rice Bowl

Ingredients

  • 1/2 can of black beans
  • 1 cup of cauliflower rice
  • 2/3 cup of mixed veggies
  • 1/2 cup of peppers
  • 1/3 cup of mushrooms
  • Herbs and spices of your choice (I use black pepper, tumeric, and cajun)
  1. Cook veggies in a skillet pan on medium heat for 8 minutes
  2. Cook cauliflower rice in a skillet pan on medium for 4 minutes
  3. Cook beans until they begin to simmer
  4. Add all contents into a bowl and mix

Peanut Butter Banana Oatmeal

Ingredients

  • 1/2 ripe banana
  • 1/2 cup of oats
  • 1/2 cup of dairy-free milk
  • 1 tbs. of cinnimon
  1. Mash banana in bottom of bowl
  2. add in oats and milk
  3. Microwave for 1 minute
  4. Add cinnimon and stir
